                                                     FLOWER  SHOP

	Flower shop app used to order flowers online.
	Consumers can place fresh flower orders on the go in few simple steps.
	2 types of users: clients and admin.
	The admin is responsible for adding the floral arrangement and the price.
	The client can browse through the app to see the available floral arrangements and also to make an order. 
	In an order, the client can choose the type of flowers, the number and a day when they want it finished.
	Auxiliary , a chat is used to communicate with the flourist to describe the desired bouquet.
